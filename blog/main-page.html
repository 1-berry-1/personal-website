That's me! in the flesh pixels
Yes, that thing on my neck is a birthmark.

Bhoomika here, and yes, I'm a 'role' as is said in Hindi. A saying goes, "the world's a stage and all of us are mere actors in it." By being an actor, one has varied faces in their lifetime: be it a daugther, a helping hand, a listener, a drunk babbler, or a joyous child. That would describe some parts of me.

I believe writing is an accompaniment to one's life. I believe one develops their opinions and perspectives through putting it in material. I believe true insight is the essence of experience, and that's invaluable - something that every human possesses uniquely. It's an art on its own - how do you communicate the same insight you have with others? How do you engage their senses in the same way yours was?

Writing is a skill, and so is conveying information. In this blog I'd like to explore how best I can do that, with experiences from my life.
