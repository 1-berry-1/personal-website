Hammer replacing woodpeckers
Trashy music blasted through the void, drowning the chirps and tweets
The air carrying the frustrated horns and revving engines
The land brown and the eternal winter trees
'the world isn't what it was, it's impure!'
Does it matter?
No, does it matter that we, in our conquest for greatness,
have forsaken
what our earth represents?
Forsaken what greatness we were born into,
in search of an imagined victory?
'Development', one says
Have all of us forgotten what our heart yearns for,
drunk with the lies our mind whispers
